How can I help you?

If any of these challenges keep you up at night (or just stuck at the office ‘til midnight), you need Steinherz Media.

Blogs and Articles

Want to get more eyes on your posts? Awesome, that's my specialty. Let's talk. I've written over 500 blogs and articles for over 30 tech companies with a focus on cyber security and data privacy.

Case Studies

Nothing says “...and here’s your proof!” like a killer case study. Case studies are the best way to showcase your solutions at work and deliver an impactful story.

Web Copy

You know what they say; you’ve only got one chance to make an awesome first impression. That’s why the words on your website matter. Really matter. Anyone can throw words up on a screen and--fingers crossed--hope it’s effective. But strategic copy is so much more than that.

Whitepapers, Solution Briefs, Press Releases

Your brand needs all these types of content collateral to impress potential buyers and investors. But few and far-between are the teams that can produce them with the resources and people they've got on-hand.

Contact Strategy

So Tal from R&D emptied her thoughts into a Google Doc and now you want to publish it to your blog? That’s nice….but how does it fit into your content strategy? Does this piece support your long term objectives and your strategic vision? Oh, what’s that you say? You've got no strategy and no clue? Let's change that!

Team Training

Do you want to make sure your content excels? In just a few hours, your entire team can learn how to create content that resonates with your audience. Build a strategy that works, learn how to stick to, and measure, it and tell stories that move your readers to act.

Editing and Proofreading

Nothing kills momentum like a spelling error. Don't believe me? Just ask my client who (before they hired me!) submitted an unedited proposal to a major enterprise and lost the deal--over a spelling mistake. Now nothing escapes my red pen.
